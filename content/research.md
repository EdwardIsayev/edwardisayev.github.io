My research is centered on the Gromov--Witten theory of Calabi--Yau hypersurfaces, especially the structural predictions that arise from the BCOV conjectures.

In particular, I work with singular hypersurfaces in weighted projective space, and aim to establish their higher-genus structure by purely algebro-geometric means. I am especially interested in Yamaguchi--Yau polynomiality, the holomorphic anomaly equation and orbifold regularity.

For a gentle introduction to this area, the [Expository Notes](https://edwardisayev.github.io/notes/) section of my website offers various short notes on the relevant prerequisites, such as orbifold cohomology and the \\(I\\)-function.
