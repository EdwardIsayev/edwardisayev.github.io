### Orbifold Cohomology (Forthcoming)
 Notes from my attempts to learn Chen-Ruan cohomology. Filled with examples, especially various weighted projective spaces. These notes also explore the combinatorial description of Chen-Ruan Cohomology, in the sense of [Coates-Iritani-Jiang](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.0024).
