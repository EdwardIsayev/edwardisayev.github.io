### Expository Notes
* **Gromov Witten Theory** (Forthcoming) - Notes from a Spring 2026 course taught by Qile Chen. Topics include the fundamentals of $\overline{\mathcal{M}}_{g,n}(X,\beta)$, the string, dilaton and divisor equations, Frobenius manifolds, the Topological Recursion Relation, and Givental's J-function.  
* **Orbifold Cohomology** (Forthcoming) - Notes from my attempts to learn Chen-Ruan cohomology. Filled with examples, especially various weighted projective spaces. These notes also explore the combinatorial description of Chen-Ruan Cohomology, in the sense of [Coates-Iritani-Jiang](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.0024)
