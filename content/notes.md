### Equivariant Orbifold Cohomology (Forthcoming)
 Notes on the practical ways of computing torus-equivariant Chen--Ruan cohomology for toric stacks, such as weighted projective space in the sense of [Coates--Iritani--Jiang](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.0024). In particular, they explore the combinatorial description of certain toric Deligne--Mumford stacks, and using this data, compute the full torus-equivariant cohomology ring. These concepts are worked out in full for \\(\mathbb{P}(1,2,2)\\).
