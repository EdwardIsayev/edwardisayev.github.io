Hello! I am Edward Isayev, a 3rd year PhD student in the Mathematics Department of Boston College. My research interests are in algebraic geometry, especially the enumerative geometry that arises from predictions in mathematical physics for certain Calabi--Yau hypersurfaces. I'm very fortunate to be advised by [Qile Chen](https://sites.google.com/bc.edu/qile-chen/).

Before BC, I was an undergraduate master's (MMath) student at the University of Edinburgh, from which I graduated with First-Class Honours in July 2024. I am originally from [Ireland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dublin).
